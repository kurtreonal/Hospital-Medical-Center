ABOUT US

The Cavite State University Medical Center is in charge of delivering basic medical services that are promotive, preventive, and curative. It aims to provide quality, affordable, and accessible health services to its patients.

Mission

To provide the highest attained patient care by making “the right to health” a reality through: delivering quality, affordable, and accessible medical services for community health awareness promoting better medical services in the workplace.

Vision

Health is a basic human right. A continuous set of quality services must be provided to ensure the reality of this right,not only to the University's academic community but also to the residents of Bacoor and nearby municipalities.
