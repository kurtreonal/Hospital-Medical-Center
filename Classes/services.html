ABOUT CvSUMED OFFERED SERVICES

The CvSU Medical service is basically a supportive unit of University. It is in charged with the delivery of the basic medical services which are promotive, preventive, and curative and to some extent rehabilitative. Its vital role is towards the achievements of delivering quality, affordable accessible medical services with maximum enjoyment of good health to its patients.


Its services are also extensively utilized by the University for its student practicum, on the job training and in varying degrees for health manpower development and research as well as for rural development program.
